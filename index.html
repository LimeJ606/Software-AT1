What is JAM?

As you can see above, JAM stands for Jesus At Marsden. JAM is the Christian lunchtime group that runs every Thursday lunch. It's a way for students to come together to learn about Jesus.

Where is JAM?

JAM runs in the classroom N3.21

Can I eat lunch during JAM?

Of course! Bring some food and join us as we learn about God.
